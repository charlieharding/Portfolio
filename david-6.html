What is Apollo 13

The player's objective is to direct the rover known as David 6 through a series of increasing complex puzzles. In each puzzle David must move to a specific spot on the other side of the level without straying from the safety of the path.

Where the coding methodologies feature is in how the player controls David. In the game's story David's distance and power limitation means that communication has to be staggered and efficient. Like a programmer writing a script, the player compiles all the commands David needs to make it through the level. David then becomes the executing machine, running through the asynchronous commands. With the addition of more complex commands such as functions and conditional logic the player becomes familiarised with a coding mentality.
