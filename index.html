Drive

Drive is a website created to help young New Zealanders get their driver’s licence at the same time as becoming safer drivers.

Drive.govt.nz

What is Apollo 13

Drive is an interactive stage show based on the fateful mission in which three astronauts were trapped 200,000 miles from home. The show takes place in a theatre like any other stage show but unlike your typical show the audience members aren’t positioned in rows of seats.

Instead audience is behind computer consoles, replicas of 1970s NASA mission control consoles. They’re organised into teams such as TELMU (lunar module electric and environmental systems) and FIDO (flight dynamics) and they’ll have to work together within these teams to solve challenges unique to their team.
